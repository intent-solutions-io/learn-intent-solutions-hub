The certification, and how to clear it.

The Claude Certified Architect — Foundations (CCAR-F) is Anthropic's first official technical certification, launched March 12, 2026. Anthropic's exam code changed from CCA-F to CCAR-F in July 2026 when three additional exams launched alongside it — if you've seen "CCA-F" elsewhere, it's the same exam under its original code.

Official verified

How to study

A diagnostic loop

  1. Take a full timed practice test cold.
  2. Score it by domain — the domain split is the signal, the total is vanity.
  3. For every miss, log which anti-pattern the trap answer represented.
  4. Patch only the weakest domain.
  5. Repeat until two consecutive tests clear 800 with no domain below ~70%.
Discipline Under a production scenario, when two answers look right, pick the one that fails safe.
