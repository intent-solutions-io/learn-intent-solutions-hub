Evaluation (21%) + workflow design (16%) + governance (15%) is over half the exam — this tests judgment about when to trust, verify, or escalate Claude's output, not just prompting technique.

Who it's for
  • Professionals using Claude as a core productivity tool — Projects, Artifacts, and workflow-based interactions, not just one-off Q&A.
  • Roles across operations, marketing, project management, education, communications, and consulting.
  • Positioned between casual AI prompt users and technical AI practitioners — not intended for software developers or ML/AI specialists (see the Developer and Architect tracks instead).
  • Regular, hands-on experience using Claude in a professional setting is recommended, not required.
How to study

A diagnostic loop

  1. Take a full timed practice test cold.
  2. Score it by domain — the domain split is the signal, the total is vanity.
  3. For every miss, note whether it was a knowledge gap or a judgment call you rushed.
  4. Patch only the weakest domain.
  5. Repeat until two consecutive tests clear 800 with no domain below ~70%.
Discipline When an output looks confident, verify before you trust it — the exam rewards catching what Claude got wrong, not just using it fluently.
